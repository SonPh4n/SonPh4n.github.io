Cooking Recipes to Build Your Soft Skills

Beginner Recipe 1

Important Techniques

  • Wash rice under cold water until clear to remove excess starch.
  • Properly layer ingredients in the cooker for even cooking and flavor distribution.
  • Gently fluff cooked rice with a fork to separate grains and integrate ingredients.
  • Add fresh garnishes such as sliced scallions for added flavor and presentation.
  • Step 1. Rinse rice under cold running water until water runs clear, about 1 minute. Drain well and transfer to bowl of a rice cooker.
  • Step 2. Stir in broth, tomatoes, garlic, Creole seasoning, pepper, and cayenne (if using).
  • Step 3. Layer chopped bell pepper and onion on top, then place sliced sausage on top.
  • Step 4. Place rice cooker bowl in rice cooker, switch rice cooker on, and cook according to manufacturer's directions until rice cooker switches itself off.
  • Step 5. Carefully remove bowl from rice cooker, fluff rice with a fork and stir all ingredients together.
  • Step 6. Cover bowl with a lid or plate and set aside to steam for 5 minutes.
  • Step 7. Divide rice mixture between two bowls and garnish with sliced scallions.
